You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are five evolution stones below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score
You win the game by matching your total score to the target score, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number
The value of each crystal is hidden and when a specific crystal is cliked, where your score reaches the target, your Eevee will transform.
